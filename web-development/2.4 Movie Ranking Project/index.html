The Best Movies according to Joba

My top 4 movies of all time.

Dune: Part 2

Currently my favourite movie ever. It was one of the few movies where I got goosebumps multiple times throughout the movie. The acting as well as the plot was so gripping. I enjoyed every minute of it, especially as the expectations going into it was so high since it was well received on a global scale. It matched - and potentially exceeded - my expectations.

Inception

Conceputally thrilling. It was one of the first movies that I watched that really made me think, and not just watched for entertainment.

Batman: The Dark Knight

Rest In Peace to the greatest joker of all time, Heath Ledger. The top 3 are interchangeable, I love well crafted superpower movies and this one tops the list by some serious distance (shoutout to Spiderman: Across The Spiderverse).

The Incredibles

I have always loved superhero films and this was one of the best and earliest of that genre that I watched. However, this pick is subject to change. I have around 10 that rotates in my top 4 list. Other notable mentions include Rise of The Guardians, Pulp Fiction, Moana, The Greatest Showman and Turbo.
